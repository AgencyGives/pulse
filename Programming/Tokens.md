Tokens are the indivisible units a [[scanner]][![Define this term](https://www.cs.fsu.edu/~engelen/courses/COP402003/define.gif)](https://www.cs.fsu.edu/~engelen/courses/COP402003/board.html#scanner) of a [[compiler]] produces for further analysis by the [[parser]]. Example tokens are programming language keywords, operators, identifiers, numbers, and punctuation.
